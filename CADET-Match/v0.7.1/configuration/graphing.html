GraphingÂ¶

Graphing in CADETMatch is done asyncronously from the normal running however it still takes computing time to generate the graphs. There is a tradeoff between how frequently graphs are generated to see the progress and how much progress is slowed by generating the graphs. Graphs are generated at the end of a generation/step during estimation/error modeling. If the time in seconds since the last time graphs where generated at the end of a step than the specified time then the graphs are generated again and the time is reset.

All times are in seconds.
